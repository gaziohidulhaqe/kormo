        Frequently Asked Questions

        Frequently Asked Questions provide concise answers to common inquiries, streamlining information access for users.

        What is the customer care number of Kormi24?

        For any of your queries, go to the contact page, and describe your question/problem. Or you can email/phone your queries to this address: info@kormi24.com, Mobile : 01615621795

        How do I upload photos to Kormi24?

        Log in to Kormi24, or if you’re new to Kormi24, then Sign up. And then go to your account profile. From there you can upload a photo from your device to set as your profile photo.

        How to create an account on Kormi24?

        To create an account on Kormi24 for the first time, go to the Sign Up page. Enter your phone number, and click the “Submit” button. An OTP (One-Time Password) will be sent to your provided phone number. You have to enter the correct OTP in the verification box and click “Submit”. Alternatively, you can also sign up for Kormi24 via your Facebook or Google account. Once you have signed up, choose an option between “Employer” and “Worker” from the pop-up. Then enter your details in the form, upload your profile photo, and get started with Kormi24.

        How do I find a worker on Kormi24?

        To find a worker on Kormi24, you can search by job categories. For example, if you are looking for an electrician, go to the search bar, and type “electrician”. To find a worker in a specific area, you can also filter your search by selecting an area or city. The search result will show you electricians available on Kormi24. From there, you can find information about individual workers on Kormi24.

        How do I get jobs from Kormi24?

        To find jobs based on your skills on Kormi24 follow these steps: - Log in or sign up to Kormi24. - Select your profile as a “Worker”. - Set up your account profile with your photo and details. - Add your skills. When an employer on Kormi24 finds you suitable for the job, they’ll hire you. For more details on how to create your account profile on Kormi24, please visit here.

        How does the payment system work on Kormi24?

        After your employer reaches out to you through Kormi24, you'll have a chance to chat with them about the job details and how you'll get paid.

        What type of jobs can I find on Kormi24?

        You can find a wide range of jobs on Kormi24 including Doctor, Nurse, Engineer, Construction worker, Mason, Web Designer, and many more. Find all job categories on Kormi24 from here - https://kormi24.com/en

        Is Kormi24 free to use?

        Yes, all Kormi24 services are free to use.

        What is on-demand online staffing?

        On-demand online staffing emphasizes immediate access to workers for urgent or short-term needs, often leveraging technology to streamline the hiring process. Kormi24 stands out as a free online staffing platform that combines the best of both worlds. It provides businesses and individuals with a comprehensive marketplace to connect with skilled workers for projects of all durations, whether short-term gigs or long-term contracts. With its user-friendly interface and robust communication tools, Kormi24 facilitates seamless interactions between employers and workers, ensuring efficient collaboration and successful outcomes. Whether you're looking for on-demand staffing solutions or seeking to build a reliable workforce for your projects, Kormi24 offers a versatile and cost-effective platform to meet your needs.
